Why Coding?!

A little bit about why I've decided to pursue coding

I've always had a love for creativity, but my skills aren't in things like singing or drawing or dancing, but rather in the technical creativity, where there is this beautiful overlap of logic and chaos.

I appreciate using my hands to build things, I enjoy capturing the moment behind a camera, I have endless ideas of creating icons using shapes, I always appreciated playing instruments/reading sheet music, and lately, have found I love spending hours behind a screen building a website. While doing any of the above activities, I find that the hours pass by so fast. I get lost in the moment.

For me, coding is an outlet for using my techical background and capabilities combined with my love of design and creativity. I'm a fan of facing problems, and logically working through a way to solve it, maybe that's just the engineer in me. Best of all, as with most things in the world, in coding there is no single right way to solve things. The options are endless, which allows the creative mind to go wild!

BACKSTORY

I was born in the Netherlands, grew up in Orlando, and have now lived in Chicago for 10+ years.

Both my parents were immigrants to the US: one from the Netherlands, one from Colombia. This unqiue mix of cultures has created a passion for travel, love of cultural diversity, and desire to expand my horizons.

I went to school here in Chicago at Northwestern, and studied to be an Industrial Engineer, and have spent the past 7 years working at Nielsen as an Operations Manager

I'm fluent in Spanish, Dutch, and currently working on learning Arabic
